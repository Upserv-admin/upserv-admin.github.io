    Scenarios

    Recognize the symptom. Then fix the layer behind it.

    Most teams recognize the symptom. Few fix the layer causing it.

    • Copy leak
      People visit, but demos aren’t happening.
      Traffic is arriving, but the message is not creating enough clarity or intent to move people forward. This usually points to a messaging or positioning breakdown before intent is formed.
    • UX / sales leak
      Interest is there, but deals aren’t closing.
      Demos happen, but momentum drops before conversion because the path from interest to decision has too much friction. This usually points to a sales-path or UX breakdown after intent already exists.
    • Onboarding leak
      Users sign up, but don’t stay.
      New accounts convert, but early product experience does not create value fast enough to prevent churn. This usually points to an activation or onboarding breakdown after signup.
    • Healthy funnel
      The funnel is stable. Now you can compound it.
      Core conversion and retention metrics are healthy, so the next move is leverage, not repair. This usually means the system is ready for pricing, expansion, or efficiency gains.
